# Auto Furnace

Design: <a href="https://www.youtube.com/@mysticat" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer">mysticat</a>

Overall, this farm was not essential. But, it definitely helped get many building blocks such as glass and stone bricks. Basically, you place the fuel at the bottom chest, and the burnables at the top. Then, turning on the lever causes the carts to flow and evenly distribute the items and fuel. The storage system then sucks the items out and stores them in the chest.
